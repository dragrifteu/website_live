MADRID
The Father (or Mother) of the whole spectacle!

Madrid is the visionary behind Dragrift – a creator, performer, and community builder who set out to elevate drag culture in Slovakia. With a deep love for artistic expression, freedom, and inclusion, he transformed a bold idea into one of the country’s most iconic queer events.

Madrid is not just the producer of the show – he is the driving force of creativity and connection. His work blends sharp visuals with powerful messaging, celebrating individuality while uniting the community. From the very first spark of an idea to the final round of applause, he brings passion, precision, and pure fabulousness into every detail of Dragrift.e

Dragrift is not just an event – it’s a statement. And Madrid is its heart.
