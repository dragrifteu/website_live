If you wanna ask

What is Dragrift?

Dragrift is the boldest celebration of drag art in Slovakia – a colorful event ruled by creativity, self-expression, and unforgettable performances. It's more than just a show. It's a movement. A space where diversity isn’t just welcome – it’s celebrated. A runway for every queen and a moment where you can be your true self – unapologetically. Dragrift isn’t just about watching – it’s about belonging. Expect sickening outfits, jaw-dropping acts, and a community that’ll boost your energy. If you're ready for a night to remember – we’ll see you front row.

Do I have to be a drag queen to come?

Absolutely not! Dragrift is for everyone – whether you're ready to serve a look, slay the stage, or scream your lungs out from the audience. All you need is the vibe and the urge to get swept off your (high) heels.
Dragrift welcomes anyone with an open mind and love for diversity.

I'm not queer – will I fit in?

Of course! Dragrift welcomes anyone with an open mind and a heart ready to celebrate uniqueness. It doesn’t matter who you are or how you identify – it’s all about respect, fun, and shared vibes. If you’re into glitter, performances, and good energy, you're one of us.

When and where does Dragrift take place?

At least once a year in Slovakia, most often in Bratislava – always packed with drama, neons, and slay moments. Follow our socials or check this site so you don’t miss a thing!

Can I sign up as a performer?

Yaaas, queen! If you're a drag artist and dream of slaying the most iconic stage around, follow our Instagram – we post performer calls several months in advance.

Can I take photos and videos?

Yes – but with respect! Tag us, spread the love, but remember not everyone wants to be filmed – especially performers off-stage. If you're unsure, just ask. And if you're a pro with a camera – contact us ahead of time.
