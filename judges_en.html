Our Judges

Pandora
Nox

Austrian drag sensation and winner of the first-ever Drag Race Germany, making her the first cis woman to snatch a crown in Drag Race herstory. She serves jaw-dropping dance, contortion, and runway eleganza. Her role as head judge at Dragrift Freaky Cirque Edition marked a historic moment as the Slovak drag scene connected with the Drag Race franchise for the very first time.

Chichi Tornado

An unstoppable force of drag — a fierce whirlwind of glamour, wit, and pure chaos. With electrifying stage presence, razor-sharp humor, and looks that slay from dusk till dawn, she turns every performance into a full-blown spectacle. ChiChi Tornado doesn’t just perform — she takes over the stage, leaving audiences breathless, gagged, and begging for more.

Spizy G

Our only regular judge, first hitting the stage at Dragrift in 2023 and quickly becoming an unstoppable force. Known as “The Mexican Storm” and “The Mexican Dragon”, she’s performed across four countries in under a year, snatched titles like Vice Miss Drag CZ SK and Misster Vienna Pride 2024*, and became the first Slovak queen signed to global drag agency Goudie Events.

Valerie
Divine

Hungarian drag diva and winner of Drag Queen Hungary 2020, making her first judging appearance at Dragrift Freaky Cirque Edition. A true icon of the Budapest scene, serving glamour, showmanship, and the power to entertain from bingo nights to the main stage.

Creative Swine
(Jakub Procházka)

Czech drag powerhouse serving high wigs, sickening looks, and show-stopping performances. In Prague, she turns it out with her solo show “Když láska schází” and snatches the crowd at every drag event worth its glitter. Served as a judge at Dragrift Dizzney and made a fierce comeback for Dragrift Freaky Cirque Edition.

Soňa
Norisová

Soňa Norisová is a renowned Slovak actress with achievements across both Czech and Slovak cinema. In Slovak dubbing, she lent her voice to Disney legends such as Pocahontas, Kida, and Megara, later also Ursula and the Evil Queen. Her appearance on the judging panel marked the first connection between Dragrift’s underground roots and the commercial sphere, and for the first time in history, a judge witnessed a lipsync battle to one of her own songs. She was a judge at the Dizzney edition of Dragrift.

Roman Samotný

Founder of the Bratislava venue Tepláreň and long-time LGBTI+ activist, who has become a symbol of courage and the fight for equality in Slovakia. His work is dedicated to community visibility and fostering open dialogue about acceptance and respect in society. He joined the judging panel at the Demon & Disney edition of Dragrift.

La Cuntessa

Slovak drag performer with a theatrical background, celebrated for her underground madness, camp aesthetic, and strong activist message. Her performances combine theatricality, humor, and provocation, creating an unmistakable experience every time. She was the very first drag queen to join the judging panel, making her debut at the Demon edition of Dragrift.

Kristína Kanátová

Slovak film actress (Netflix drama Fentasy), who has never been afraid to stand up for human rights and values while highlighting the importance of mental health and overall well-being. She appeared as a judge at the Slovak edition of Dragrift.

Braňo
Fidler

Activist and co-founding member of the queer sports club Lotosové kvety in Bratislava, whose vision is to create safe sporting spaces for the community. He joined the judging panel at the very first Queen edition of Dragrift.

Lady
Vivian

Lady Vivian is a Slovak actress and one of the prominent figures of the burlesque scene. Her work blends theatricality, glamour, and a strong focus on body positivity, making her stand out in the alternative arts scene. She was the very first female judge at the first Queen edition and later joined the panel again at the second Tropical edition of Dragrift.

Milan
WTF

One of the most renowned makeup artists on our scene and a prominent influencer, supporting Dragrift as one of the very first judges at Tropic Haus Edition. Known for precision, trend-setting looks, and turning makeup into a true experience.
